Bhiwandi, Maharashtra

Location

Bhiwandi is a city, near Thane city in the Thane district in Maharashtra, India. It is located 20 kilometres northeast of Mumbai and 15 km northeast of the city of Thane.

About

Weather

Bhiwandi experiences a mild and dry winter (15°C - 28°C) from December to February, followed by a hot pre-monsoon period (20°C - 40°C) from March to May. The monsoon season (June - September) brings heavy rainfall, while the post-monsoon months (October - November) offer milder temperatures (20°C - 33°C) and less rainfall.

Population

Bhiwandi's population was around 9,77,000 people

Sports

Sports in Bhiwandi encompass a range of activities including cricket, football, and kabaddi, with local clubs and tournaments being popular.

Things To Do

  • Visit the historic landmarks.
  • Explore the beautiful parks and nature reserves.
  • Try out the local cuisine and restaurants.
